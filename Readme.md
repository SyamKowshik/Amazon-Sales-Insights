Project: Amazon Sales Analytics and Visualization (Tableau)

Story : The project unfolds a visual exploration of Amazon sales data through meticulously designed Tableau dashboards. These dashboards encapsulate a detailed and dynamic representation of various facets of the sales dataset, offering a comprehensive view of trends, patterns, and essential performance indicators.

The significance of this topic lies in its ability to transform raw data into visually compelling insights. By leveraging the power of Tableau, the document not only provides a visual feast of graphs and charts but also serves as a strategic tool for decision-makers. It allows stakeholders to discern and analyze the intricacies of Amazon sales, ultimately aiding in the formulation of well-informed business strategies.

In a world inundated with data, the visual analytics presented in this document become a critical asset for unraveling the complexities of the Amazon sales landscape. The interactivity and dynamic nature of Tableau dashboards enhance the user experience, enabling users to delve deep into specific areas of interest, identify emerging trends, and extract actionable insights for optimizing business performance.

Set Up: This project is a presentation to various sales executives and interested people. The presentation will start explain the above introduction and then dive into the data sets and to the dashboards. The dashboards are created to grab the attention of the audience and ease of understanding.

Dashboards: Introduction:

The logical sequence of the dashboards has been carefully structured to provide a coherent narrative and facilitate a step-by-step exploration of key insights within the Amazon sales dataset. The order is designed to progressively uncover valuable information, starting with customer preferences, moving to shipping dynamics, and concluding with an overall statistical overview.

Order of Dashboards:

Dashboard-1: Clothing Type Interest by State This dashboard allows users to explore and understand the distribution of sales quantities across various clothing categories within each state. It provides insights into regional preferences, helping users identify which types of clothing are particularly popular in specific states.

Dashboard-2: Shipping Modes Statistics by State This dashboard provides insights into the relationship between shipping modes, quantity of sales, and the status of products within different states. Users can explore patterns and trends related to how customers in various states prefer different shipping options, and whether there's a correlation between shipping preferences and product status.

Dashboard-3: Quantity and Amount of Sales Statistics This dashboard offers a comprehensive view of the quantity and amount of sales, allowing users to analyze trends over time, understand the impact of product categories and sizes on sales, and explore how shipping status influences these metrics. It enables users to derive insights into the dynamics of sales based on various dimensions, fostering a deeper understanding of the overall sales landscape.

Dashboard-4: Used for Conclusion of presentation It include worksheets from previous dashboards in addition with text graphs of total sales, Amount, Quantities, categories, B2B Sales, Sales channel and courier status graphs.

Conclusion: The chosen order of dashboards ensures a logical progression from understanding customer preferences to exploring shipping dynamics and finally providing an overall statistical snapshot. Each dashboard is strategically crafted to answer specific questions, employing a variety of chart types to offer nuanced insights into the Amazon sales dataset.<img
